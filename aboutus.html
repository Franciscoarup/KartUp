Who Are We?


KartUp is an online E-Commerce platform that serves out basic everyday needs. The website aims at customer accessibility and give them utmost priority also provide them with comfort of online shopping in different categories like Fresh Basket, Daily Needs and many more to explore. It all started with a group of five enthusiastic college mates working on their college projects and while working they discovered their interest and thought of carrying along, with aiming for greater perspective and vision in the field of E-Commerce. The website is created and owned by Samadreeta Mukherjee, Arup Mandal, Anwesha Bhowmick, Preet Agarwala and Nikhil Kumar Bhagat. At the end we would like to wish our customers a happy and smart buying. Happy Karting!
